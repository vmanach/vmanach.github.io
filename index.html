About Me

I am a first-year PhD student at Télécom Paris - Institut Polytechnique de Paris, within the S2A team of the LTCI laboratory. My research is supervised by Prof. Pavlo Mozharovskyi, Prof. Stephan Clémençon, and Prof. Winston Maxwell.

Research Topic

My research focuses on developing a statistical framework for understanding and quantifying bias in artificial intelligence systems. It explores the use of statistical inference methods—such as hypothesis testing and confidence intervals—to characterize deviations from fairness in machine learning models, both at global and local levels. Grounded in the regulatory context of the European Union’s AI Act, this work seeks to contribute to the design of more transparent, reliable, and fair AI systems.
